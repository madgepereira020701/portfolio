Gymnasium Management System



Description


The Gymnasium Management System involves two main entities: administrators (including managers) and members (including trainers).

For Administrators:

Account Management: Log in with existing credentials or create a new account.

Gym Management:

Manage members and trainers.

Handle fee details, memberships, and equipment.

View and update payment details, membership records, and gym details.

Track and update equipment rental fees, quantity, and availability.

Update gym contact information, operating hours, and member/trainer statistics.

Members:

Account Management: Log in with existing credentials or create a new account.

Personal Details:

View membership type, payment status, personal trainers, rented equipment, and gym details.

The system facilitates comprehensive management and tracking of gym operations for both administrators and members.

Technologies used:

C#, Oracle, MYSQL
